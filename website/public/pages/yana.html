The mental health bot that provides assistance to people suffering from mental health disorders like depression, anxiety, panic attacks and more.


A bot that gives the feeling of having someone who cares hence the name YANA aka You are not alone.

YANA (You Are Not Alone) is a chatbot developed by the team ACROSSTECH, in which it not only help the students suffering from mental health problems such as depression, but also let people be informed of the current situation, that a lot of students are in need of help.
