ABOUT

The purpose and main objective of this project, which is an artificial intelligence chatbot named "AceKo," is to provide service and convenience to the students of Bicol University, as they can rely on this when seeking educational information or information within the university. It also aims to prevent the dissemination of misleading information to students regarding news, updates, or announcements from the university.

This chatbot, "AceKo," is available exclusively to Bicol University students and is accessible 24/7. This chatbot can stand as a virtual student buddy for every Bicol University student. Whenever the students have queries, they can just access "AceKo," and it will be there for them. This chatbot can also be considered a school information system (SIS).
